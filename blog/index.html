How it all begun.....

Hey all. I'm sure you're all wondering how it all started......

It is not very complicated story. I never really liked bikes. They seamed uncomfortable and pointless. In the end why would you sit out in the open, almost no protection of any kind (and that's assuming that you are wearing your protective gear..), either sweating on every stop or constantly freezing and yet.........

One day when it took me 3hrs by car to get home for a jurney which normally takes 45min walk I've noticed that bikes just squized through beetwen cars and went on the way. Same with parking - at that time I had almost always empty MC parking space in front of my doors.

All of this together and one day I decided to give it a go and try the bikes. From this point on - all is history.

You can read more about my story in the blog below.
